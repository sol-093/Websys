**Target Audience:** Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmarinas (KLD) Administration and Leadership  
**Purpose of Pitch:** To advocate for the implementation of a centralized management system that addresses chronic transparency and accountability issues in KLD student organizations.

---

## COMPLETE MANUSCRIPT/SCRIPT

### Hook (10–15 seconds)

Over the past years, how many times have KLD student organizations faced questions about where club funds went? How many events were cancelled because of mismanaged budgets? The students of Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmarinas deserve to know that their organization fees are being used responsibly and transparently.

### Problem (15 seconds)

Our institution's student organizations currently operate without centralized oversight, leading to persistent issues: unaccounted funds, lack of budget transparency, unclear financial reports, and minimal accountability structures. Past incidents have eroded student trust—organizations have failed to provide transparent accounting of collected fees, expenditures remain opaque, and there's no standardized reporting mechanism. This fragmented approach has created an environment where accountability falls through the cracks, and legitimate organizational concerns go unaddressed.

### Solution (20 seconds)

We propose INVOLVE—a centralized student organization management and budget transparency system designed specifically for KLD. INVOLVE provides a unified platform where organizations can document all financial transactions in real-time, submit regular budget reports through a standardized format, and maintain auditable records accessible to both leadership and student stakeholders. The system includes automated transparency features: budget allocation tracking, expense categorization, member contribution logging, and monthly financial statements. Administrators gain oversight capabilities to ensure compliance, while students gain visibility into how their organization operates.

### Value Proposition (15 seconds)

What makes INVOLVE unique is that it's built on trust and accountability without creating bureaucratic burden. Unlike manual processes, it automates compliance and reduces administrative overhead. Organizations spend less time on paperwork and more time on their mission. For the institution, it mitigates financial risk and protects institutional reputation. Most importantly, it restores student confidence by making financial management transparent, verifiable, and fair—turning accountability from a source of distrust into a source of institutional pride.

### Closing Call to Action (10 seconds)

We urge Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmarinas to adopt INVOLVE. By implementing this system now, we set a standard of excellence in student organization governance, demonstrate commitment to accountability, and empower our students to lead with integrity. Together, we can build a stronger, more transparent KLD community.

- **Attire:** Business casual or formal
